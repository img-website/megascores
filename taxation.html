GST Deduction On Deposits
MEGASCORES deducts 28% GST on deposits as per government regulations. This deducted GST is adjusted and added back to your MEGACASH balance, ensuring you get full value for your deposits.
TDS Deduction On Winnings
MEGASCORES deducts 30% TDS on net winnings as per tax regulations. Users can track TDS deductions in their transaction history, ensuring transparency and easy access to tax details.
